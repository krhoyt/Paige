# Slow Cooker Chai Tea

**Preparation time:** 20 minutes

**Cook time:** 8 hours

**Total time:** 8 hours, 20 minutes

## Ingredients

- 15 slices fresh ginger root (about 3 ounces)
- 3 cinnamon sticks (3 inches)
- 25 whole cloves
- 15 cardamom pods, lightly crushed
- 3 whole peppercorns
- 3 1/2 quarts water
- 8 black tea bags
- 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk

## Instructions

1. Place the first 5 ingredients on a double thickness of cheesecloth. Gather corners of cloth to enclose seasonings; tie securely with string. Place spice bag and water in a 5 or 6 quart slow cooker. Cook, covered, on low for 8 hours. Discard spice bag.

2. Add tea bags; steep, covered, 3–5 minutes according to taste. Discard tea bags. Stir in milk; heat through. Serve warm.
