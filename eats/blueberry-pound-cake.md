# Blueberry Pound Cake

**Preparation time:** 20 minutes  

**Cook time:** 1 hour 10 minutes  

**Cool time:** 10 minutes

**Total time:** 1 hours 40 minutes

## Ingredients

- 2 cups granulated sugar  
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/2 cup (4-ounces) packages cream cheese, softened  
- 3 large eggs  
- 1 large egg white  
- 3 cups all-purpose flour, divided  
- 2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries  
- 1 teaspoon baking powder  
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda  
- 1/2 teaspoon salt  
- 1 (8-ounce) lemon yogurt  
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract  
- Cooking spray  
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar  
- 4 teaspoons lemon juice  

## Instructions

*If you’re using frozen blueberries, do not thaw them before adding them to the batter.*

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).  

2. Combine the sugar, butter, and cream cheese in a large bowl; beat at medium speed of a mixer for 5 minutes or until well blended. Add eggs and egg white, one at a time, beating well after each addition.  

3. Combine 2 tablespoons flour and blueberries in a small bowl; toss well.  

4. Combine the remaining flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl; stir well.  
   
5. Add the flour mixture alternately with yogurt to the sugar mixture, beginning and ending with flour mixture. Fold in the blueberry mixture and vanilla.  

6. Pour batter into a 10-inch tube pan coated with cooking spray. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour and 10 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted in the center comes out clean.  

7. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes on a wire rack; remove from pan.  

8. Combine powdered sugar and lemon juice in a small bowl; stir well. Drizzle over warm cake. Cut with a serrated knife.
