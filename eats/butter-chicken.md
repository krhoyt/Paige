# Indian Butter Chicken

**Preparation time:** 30 minutes

**Cook time:** 6 hours on low

**Total time:** 6 hours 30 minutes  
*(but only 30 minutes of work)*

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon salted butter
- 1 cup yellow onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons ginger
- 6 oz tomato paste
- 1 tablespoon garam masala
- 1 teaspoon brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 12 oz full-fat coconut milk
- 1/2 cup chicken stock
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 2 lbs chicken thighs
- 1 cup of Jasmine rice

## InstructionsÂ 

1. Cube chicken to 1 inch cubes, and add to crock pot with diced onion.

2. In a separate mixing bowl thoroughly combine remaining ingredients to make the sauce.

3. Pour sauce into crock pot. Stir to coat chicken. 

4. Cook on low for 6 hours, stirring occasionally.

5. Cook rice prior to serving. Spoon butter chicken over rice.

## From Mom and Dad

- If you are working with a small crock pot, pour half the sauce, and then mix to coat chicken. Then pour in as much of remaining sauce as will fit in your pot. No need for a second mix until the dish has started to cook.
- You can leave out the chicken stock for a slightly thicker sauce - or if you need room in a smaller crock pot.
- We like ours spicy. To get there we use 1 teaspoon black pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes. This will yield a level-3 heat.
