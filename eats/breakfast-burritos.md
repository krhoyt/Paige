# Breakfast Burritos

**Preparation time:** 50 minutes

**Cook time:** 5 minutes (microwave)

**Total time:** 55 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1/2 lbs. ground pork sausage
- 1/2 lbs. frozen cubed Ore Ida potatoes
- 6 eggs, beaten
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 10 oz. can green chili sauce
- 6 – 8 burrito-size flour tortillas
- 1 cup cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. In large skillet, brown sausage. Remove from heat and drain excess fat. Set aside.

2. Brown potatoes in oven by placing on cookie sheet and broiling according to package directions until crisp.

3. Meanwhile, mix eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together in medium mixing bowl.

4. Over medium heat, melt butter in separate large skillet. Add egg mixture to skillet and scramble eggs, removing from heat once eggs are cooked but still moist.

5. Warm green chili over medium heat in saucepan until heated through.

6. To assemble burritos: Warm flour tortillas on skillet or griddle until flexible, turning once.

7. Fill tortillas with 1/3 cup scrambled eggs, 2–3 tablespoons sausage, and 1/3 cup potatoes. Top with cheddar cheese and green chili according to taste.

8. Fold filled tortillas into burritos.

9. Wrap each burrito in plastic wrap, then place individual burritos in freezer bags. Freeze.

## From Mom and Dad

- These are make ahead burritos. You can make the whole batch, freeze them, and then have them on the go, at the "office", etc.

- Top with salsa or other sauce for a little fresh flavor.
