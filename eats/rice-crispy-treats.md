# Jackie's Rice Crispy Treats

**Preparation time:** 20 minutes

**Cook time:** 0 minutes

**Total time:** 20 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 stick of butter
- 2 bags 10.5 oz marshmallows
- 8 oz marshmallow cream
- 1 box 12 oz Rice Crispy

## Instructions

1. In a large stock pot, combine all ingredients except the cereal. Melt until smooth (medium heat).

2. Stir in 1/2 bag of Rice Crispy cereal. Repeat with remaining 1/2 bag.

3. Put in greased pan. Gently push down until even.
