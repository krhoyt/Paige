# Taco Soup

**Preparation time:** 20 minutes

**Cook time:** 30 minutes

**Total time:** 50 minutes

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of hamburger
- 1/2 of a large onion chopped
- 2 cans pinto beans, drained
- 1 can rotel tomatoes (original flavor)
- 1 can petite diced tomatoes
- 12 oz Velveeta cubed (or more)
- 1/2 - 2/3 cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Brown the hamburger with the onion and drain.

2. Season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.

3. Add the canned vegetables.

4. Once heated through, add the Velveeta and once that was melted, add the sour cream.

## From Mom and Dad

- Some possible substitutions

  - 2 cans Cannellini beans (drained)
  - 1 can crushed tomatoes (1 lbs)
  - 1 4oz can diced green chili
