# Spicy Bistro Steak Subs

**Preparation time:** 20 minutes  

**Cook time:** 10 minutes  

**Total time:** 30 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon stick margarine
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 pound thinly sliced lean deli roast beef
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 4 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
- 1 (12-ounce) can dark beer
- 6 (2 1/2-ounce) hoagie rolls with sesame seeds, cut in half lengthwise
- Veggie tray such as carrots and celery (optional)

## Instructions

1. Melt margarine in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. 

2. Add garlic; saut√© 2 minutes. 

3. Add roast beef and next 6 ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 2 minutes, stirring frequently. 

4. Drain roast beef in a colander over a bowl, reserving sauce. Divide roast beef evenly among roll bottoms, and top with roll tops. 

5. Serve sandwiches with reserved sauce.
