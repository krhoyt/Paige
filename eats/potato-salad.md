# Potato Salad

**Preparation time:** 30 minutes

**Cook time:** 30 minutes

**Total time:** 1 hour

## Ingredients

- 5 lbs potatoes
- 1 cup onion (white)
- 1 cup celery
- 10 hard boiled eggs
- 48 oz Kraft Real Mayonnaise
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp pepper

## Instructions

1. Clean and boil potatoes with skin on until fork tender.

2. Cut into 1.5" cubes.

3. Add onion, celery, and eggs in a bowl. Mix well.

4. Add mayo, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

5. Combine mayo mixture with potatoes.

6. Add additional mayo if not creamy enough.

7. Add additional salt and pepper to taste.

## From Mom and Dad

- This recipe makes a massive batch of potato salad. Good luck even finding a bowl that will fit it all. Even half of it is good enough for ~10 cups.

- Consider small red potatoes as a slightly more glycemic-index-friendly vegetable.

- You will likely want to season with more salt and more pepper at serving.

- Potato salad is often nicely garnished with a sprinkling of paprika.

- Consider adding Dijon mustard. Start with 2 tablespoon - a little goes a long way.
