# Make-Ahead Breakfast Casserole

**Preparation time:** 20 minutes

**Chill time:** 8 - 12 hours

**Cook time:** 50 minutes

**Total time:** 9 - 13 hours  
*(but only 20 minutes of work)*

## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound lean ground pork  
- 3/4 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning  
- 1/4 teaspoon fennel seeds, crushed  
- 2 garlic cloves, minced  
- 1 cup skim milk  
- 1/4 cup (4 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese  
- 3/4 teaspoons dry mustard  
- 1/2 teaspoon salt  
- 1/2 teaspoon ground red pepper  
- 3 green onions, chopped  
- 2 (8-ounce) cartons egg substitute  
- 6 (1-ounce) slices white bread, cut into 1/2-inch cubes  
- Cooking spray  

## Instructions

1. Cook ground pork, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and minced garlic in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until pork is browned, stirring to crumble. Drain; set aside.  

2. Combine milk and the next 6 ingredients in a large bowl; stir with a whisk until well blended. Add pork mixture and bread cubes, stirring until well blended. Pour into an 11 x 7-inch baking dish coated with cooking spray. Cover and chill 8 to 12 hours.  

3. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).  

4. Bake, uncovered, at 350°F for 50 minutes or until set and lightly browned. Garnish with thyme, if desired.
